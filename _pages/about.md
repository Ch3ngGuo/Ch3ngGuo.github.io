I am a Master student at the **Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST)**, majoring in Information and Communication Engineering, with wild ambitions and diverse interests. In the future, I hope to develop an interpretable general model capable of recognizing the world by incorporating more statistical and physics-inspired knowledge (*e.g.* AI4Physics and Physics4AI) . Currently, I am doing research about <u>end2end task-aligned object detection</u>, <u>fluid-based image registration and generation</u>, and <u>polarization-based vision</u> in the State Key Multispectral Information Processing (MIP) Lab.  You can visit [Publications](/publications) to know more details.

Meanwhile, I am also looking for PhD opportunities. To learn more about me, you can have a view of my CV page [here](/files/ChengGuo_CV.pdf) and IELTS score [here](/files/IELTS.pdf).

Also, in my spare time I enjoy films, music and reading. Max Weber and Rilke are my favorite authors, A Beautiful Mind is my favorite film, and Radiohead is my favorite band.
